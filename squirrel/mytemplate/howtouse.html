Registration and Login :-

Registration

Click on Register and fill up the form . please give a valid email as we are going to send a OTP to that email and you will be ask for that later. Click on eye 👁 to see your password. we suggest to provide a totally different password for this site, which you have not used anywhere. your password will remain encrypted in our system. your email will be used only for login purposes, and your fullname will be used everywhere in your profile. after filling the form click on register

you will be asked for confirmation then click send otp , if no account with this email is found you will be redirected to new OTP page, otherwise you need to provide a different email ID.

OTP verification

if you provide a real email ,check your inbox (or spam folder) of that email, a 6 digit OTP have been sent. write (copy paste) the OTP in this form. if entered wrong OTP error will be shown in that case type again. if you have not get the OTP click the link Didn't get OTP , we will send another OTP , enter the latest OTP. you will have atmost 5 chances to enter the OTP (per day per email). we will send OTP atmost 3 times (per day per email). after successfull OTP verification we will redirect to your Dashboard (profile) page. Now you have access to all the features.

Login

if you have already registered you can login now . enter your valid email ID and password in this form and click login .you will be logged in. Your password will always remain encrypted in our system.

Profile :-

So after registration/login you will see this as the first page so called dashboard (profile) page.

Edit profile
Click on Edit profile (present in the profile page) and provide all other details like Bio,school,college , now you can change your fullname.
upload profile picture
you can change your profile picture in this Edit profile section or remove picture.
set privacy (public/private profile)
if you don't want others to see your profile or simply follow you, you can make your profile as Private. click the button make private in the edit profile section

public profile :- any verified user can follow you , can see you posts and like,can see your profile ,profile pic. your profile details (but not profile pic) will be shown in public searches.

private profile :- no one can follow you simply , only your followers and following can see your profile ,profile pic,posts and like.your profile details will not be shown in public searches.


update status

you can post any ligal content here, you can upload pictures . find it there in the update status section. this post will be available in the network page of your followers. Any verified user can see this post and like when he/she views your profile.

delete account

If you feel insecure, uncomfortable or whatever the reason, you have a option to delete you account, find it there in the edit profile section (Dashboard). All your data, profile details, all photos, all posts, chat, email ID, password everything will be deleted. however we suggest instead of this make you profile private and complain to Help Desk.
